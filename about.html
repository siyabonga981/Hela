Who we are

We are a advisory firm that helps clients with strategy projects, due diligence services, and human capital capacitation projects.

Our training division assist clients with the SAICA CA2025 program and trainee management services.

Hela Inc. is managed by a team of dynamic competent individuals with the experience to execute small and big engagements. We are committed to knowing more, sourcing experts, and professional talent while benchmarking against international trends. This in turn helps us to consider our client's best interest in providing reliable solutions.
